## Introduction

Infinity Gym is a modern, responsive fitness center website that allows users to explore membership plans, subscribe to services, and connect with the gym. The platform provides a seamless experience across all devices with its mobile-responsive design and intuitive interface.

The website showcases various membership options, trainer profiles, facility images, and offers an easy subscription process. Users can also contact the gym directly through the website's contact form.

## Prerequisites and Requirements

To use the Infinity Gym website, you need:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge - latest versions recommended)
- Internet connection
- Email address for subscription confirmation
- Phone number for subscription process

No special software installation is required. Simply open the website in your browser to get started.

## Getting Started

### Registration/Login Process

Infinity Gym does not require traditional user registration. Instead, users can directly subscribe to membership plans through the website:

1. Navigate to the "Memberships plan" section by clicking on the "Subscribe" link in the navigation bar or by scrolling down to the subscription section
2. Browse through the available membership plans (Basic, Standard, Premium)
3. Click the "Subscribe" button on your preferred plan
4. Fill out the subscription form with your details
5. Submit the form to complete your subscription request

### Navigating the Main Dashboard

The main dashboard is the homepage of the Infinity Gym website, featuring:

1. **Navigation Bar** - Located at the top with links to Home, Contact, and Subscribe sections
2. **Hero Section** - Eye-catching banner with motivational message and call-to-action button
3. **Facility Gallery** - Images showcasing the gym's modern facilities
4. **Trainer Profiles** - Information about certified trainers
5. **Contact Section** - Form to send messages to the gym
6. **Membership Plans** - Subscription options with detailed features
7. **Location and Hours** - Information about gym location and operating hours
8. **Footer** - Copyright information and contact details

To navigate between sections, either use the navigation links at the top or scroll through the page.

## Key Features and Functions

### Exploring Membership Plans

The website offers three membership plans:

1. **Basic Plan (2000DA/month)**
   - Weight training equipment access
   - Locker room facilities
   - Basic gym amenities
   - Equipment orientation
   - Standard operating hours

2. **Standard Plan (2500DA/month)**
   - Full weight training access
   - Cardio machines access
   - Premium locker facilities
   - Equipment training session
   - Extended operating hours
   - Workout progress tracking

3. **Premium Plan (3500DA/month)**
   - All Standard features included
   - Unlimited Group Classes access
   - Sauna and Wellness Area access
   - Personalized 1-on-1 Trainer Session (1/month)
   - 24/7 VIP Access
   - Nutrition Consulting Session (1/month)

To view these plans:
1. Scroll to the "Memberships plan" section
2. Review the features listed for each plan
3. Compare benefits to select the most suitable option

### Subscribing to a Plan

To subscribe to a membership plan:

1. Navigate to the "Memberships plan" section
2. Click the "Subscribe" button on your preferred plan
3. Fill out the subscription form:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Subscription Duration (1, 3, 6, or 12 months)
4. Click "Confirm & Pay" to submit your subscription request

After submission, you'll receive a confirmation message, and the gym staff will contact you to finalize the enrollment process.

### Contacting the Gym

To contact the gym:

1. Navigate to the "Get In Touch" section
2. Fill out the contact form with:
   - Your Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Message Subject
   - Detailed Message
3. Click "Send" to submit your inquiry

Alternatively, you can use the contact information provided in the footer:
- Address: 123 Fitness Street, Wellness City, FT 12345
- Phone: +1 (555) 123-4567
- Email: contact@gymcenter.com

### Accessing Admin Panel

The admin panel is available for gym staff to manage members and subscriptions:

1. Press Ctrl+Alt+A on your keyboard while on the homepage
2. The "ADMIN" link will appear in the navigation bar
3. Click the "ADMIN" link to access the admin panel

Note: This feature is only intended for authorized gym staff.

## Common Errors and Troubleshooting

### Form Submission Issues

**Problem**: Form won't submit or shows error message
**Solution**: 
1. Ensure all required fields are filled out
2. Check that email address is in valid format
3. Verify internet connection is stable
4. Refresh the page and try again

### Website Not Loading Properly

**Problem**: Website appears broken or elements are missing
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Check internet connection

### Subscription Confirmation Not Received

**Problem**: No confirmation after submitting subscription form
**Solution**:
1. Check spam/junk email folder
2. Verify email address was entered correctly
3. Wait up to 24 hours for confirmation
4. Contact gym directly using provided contact information

### Mobile Responsiveness Issues

**Problem**: Website doesn't display properly on mobile device
**Solution**:
1. Rotate device to landscape mode
2. Zoom out if page appears too large
3. Update browser to latest version
4. Try alternative browser

## FAQ

**Q: Do I need to create an account to browse membership plans?**
A: No, the website can be viewed without creating an account. Account creation happens during the subscription process.

**Q: How do I cancel my membership?**
A: To cancel your membership, please contact the gym directly using the contact information provided on the website.

**Q: Are there any additional fees beyond the monthly subscription?**
A: The prices shown on the website are monthly rates. Additional fees may apply for specific services like personal training sessions beyond what's included in your plan.

**Q: Can I switch between membership plans?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan by contacting the gym administration.

**Q: Is there a trial period available?**
A: For information about trial periods or special offers, please contact the gym directly.

**Q: How do I access the gym facilities after subscribing?**
A: After completing your subscription, the gym staff will contact you with enrollment details and provide access instructions.
